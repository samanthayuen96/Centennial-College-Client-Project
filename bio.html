About Corey J. Stone

Corey J. Stone is an Ottawa-based freelance makeup artist with almost 20 years of professional experience. He holds a certificate in make up design from Barrett Palmer International and obtained his certificate in hairstyling from Versailles Academy. Moreover, he has trained with theatrical make up and special effects artist Lizzie Ciesluk.

Before becoming freelancing, Corey honed his skills working as a beauty advisor specializing in makeup application and skin care for Clinique, Make Up For Ever, Sephora, MAC, and Estee Lauder. He is a guest lecturer at Versailles Academy for the course in Make Up Techniques.

Corey has extensive experience in makeup for weddings and destination weddings, makeup for multimedia professional and promotional photo shoots, make up for runway modelling, theatrical and stage makeup and make up for film and music videos.

He has also worked as an assistant makeup artist for made-for-TV movies produced in the Ottawa area by Zed Filmworks, Reel One Entertainment and NC Thrilling Films. In his leisure, Corey has a passion for art and theatre and to give back to his community as a volunteer make up designer for Ottawa community theatres, Ottawa Little Theatre and ToToToo.
